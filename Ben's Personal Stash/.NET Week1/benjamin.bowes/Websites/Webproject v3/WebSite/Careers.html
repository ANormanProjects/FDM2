Exciting Careers for Innovative Budgies

As a rising start-up, Budgie is always searching for fantastic developers to take on the opportunity to embark on an phenomenal journey with us to become the best budgeting app ever. Seriously.

We are looking for exciting and innovative individuals who think outside of the box and work passionately with a diverse range of people in our Budgie team in London.

You don't need any fancy degrees or certificates! If you have the developer skills were looking for, please come and show off your finesse at programming at BudgieHQ by taking part in our tests.

What does Budgie need?

Skills
  • HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • C# and Ruby
  • Excellent Teamworking Skills
  • Innovative and creative mindset
  • Fantastic Ambition for success and learning
  • Remarkable Professionalism
  • Exciting and motivating personality
  • Excellent customer service experience

We welcome anyone from any ethnic minority or backgrounds and any disability and gender. We do not discriminate so please don't be shy as your talents may be just what we were searching for!

If you are interested in joining Budgie as a developer, please email careers@budgie.com. We look forward to hearing from you soon.
